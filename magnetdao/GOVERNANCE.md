# MagnetDAO Governance

## Overview

MagnetDAO operates on a quarterly governance cycle. Token holders participate in proposal review and voting, while the Founder retains final approval authority to protect the token's value and the DAO's long-term stability.

---

## Quarterly Cycle

Each quarter follows this sequence:

1. **Proposal Submission** — Projects apply for liquidity support using the formal proposal template.
2. **Community Discussion** — Open discussion takes place in the MagnetDAO Discord. Members can ask questions, flag concerns, and build consensus.
3. **Official Vote** — At the end of the quarter, eligible proposals go to an official on-chain vote.
4. **Liquidity Deployment** — Winning proposals receive treasury-backed liquidity deployment.

---

## Proposal Requirements

All proposals must include the following:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Project Name | Name and brief description of the project |
| Liquidity Pair | The token pair being requested (e.g. PROJECT/Magnet) |
| Capital Requested | Amount of treasury funds requested |
| Expected Market Impact | How the liquidity will improve market conditions |
| Timeline | Expected deployment and lock duration |
| Known Risks | Honest assessment of project and liquidity risks |

Incomplete proposals will not advance to the voting phase.

---

## When No Projects Apply

If no projects formally submit a proposal in a given quarter:

1. **Rollover** — Treasury funds accumulated that quarter roll over into the following quarter's deployment pool.
2. **Founder Nomination** — The Founder may manually nominate projects for community consideration, which then follow the standard discussion and voting process.

---

## Voting Rules

- **Mechanism:** 1 Magnet = 1 Vote
- **Timing:** Official votes are held at the end of each quarter
- **Eligibility:** Any wallet holding Magnet tokens at the time of the vote may participate
- **Discussion:** Pre-vote discussion happens in the MagnetDAO Discord

---

## Founder Authority

MagnetDAO is a founder-led system. The Founder holds final approval authority over all liquidity decisions. This authority exists to:

- Protect the token's value from low-quality or misaligned deployments
- Ensure treasury capital is deployed responsibly
- Maintain operational stability during the DAO's early development

As the DAO matures and governance mechanisms strengthen, founder involvement in day-to-day decisions is expected to decrease while the proposal and voting system takes on greater autonomy.

---

## Accountability

The governance structure is designed to align incentives:

- Larger holders have greater voting influence but also greater exposure to poor decisions
- Proposals are evaluated publicly, creating a transparent record
- Treasury performance is visible on-chain

This creates natural accountability — participants who consistently support strong projects benefit alongside the DAO; those who support weak or misaligned projects share in the consequences.
