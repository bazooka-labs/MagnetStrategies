# MagnetDAO Treasury

## Funding Source

The MagnetDAO treasury is funded through revenue generated by Bazooka Labs' applications. A portion of this revenue is allocated to the treasury on a quarterly basis. This model provides a consistent and growing capital base without relying on token sales, inflationary emissions, or community fundraising.

---

## Quarterly Accumulation

Treasury funds accumulate each quarter in parallel with the governance cycle:

- Revenue from Bazooka Labs apps flows into the treasury
- At the end of the quarter, accumulated funds are eligible for deployment based on governance outcomes
- If no qualifying proposals are approved, funds roll over into the following quarter's pool

---

## Deployment Mechanism

When a proposal is approved, the treasury deploys liquidity as follows:

1. Treasury capital is used to acquire the winning project's token
2. That token is paired with Magnet in a liquidity pool
3. The pool is deployed on a DEX selected for optimal fee tier and market fit

DEX selection is flexible — the DAO chooses the platform and configuration that best serves each deployment's strategy and target market conditions.

---

## Founder Oversight

The Founder retains final authority over treasury deployment decisions. This ensures that capital is not deployed into projects that pose unacceptable risk to the token's value or the DAO's long-term health, regardless of vote outcomes.

---

## Transparency

All treasury deployments are executed on-chain and publicly verifiable on the Algorand blockchain. Pool positions, liquidity balances, and transaction history are accessible to any member at any time via on-chain data or the relevant DEX interface.
