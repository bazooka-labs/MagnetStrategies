## Dual Role

Magnet ($U) serves two functions within the DAO:

1. **Governance Token** — Holders vote on quarterly liquidity proposals. Voting weight is 1 token = 1 vote.
2. **Liquidity Anchor** — Magnet is the base asset in all DAO-deployed liquidity pools. Every project that receives treasury support is paired directly with Magnet.

This dual role means the token's utility grows in proportion to the depth and activity of the liquidity ecosystem it anchors.

---

## Distribution

Token distribution is not released all at once. Magnet tokens are distributed progressively as new liquidity pools are formed. This design:

- Ties supply growth to real liquidity activity rather than a pre-determined schedule
- Avoids large unlocks that could create sudden sell pressure
- Aligns token availability with the expansion of the DAO's market presence

---

## Fee Generation

Swap fees generated by Magnet liquidity pools accrue to liquidity providers. As the number of active pools grows and trading volume increases, the aggregate fee-generating capacity of the Magnet ecosystem expands. This model is intended to reinforce Magnet's value through sustained market utility rather than speculation alone.

---

## Treasury Relationship

The DAO treasury does not hold Magnet as its primary reserve asset. Treasury capital is funded externally through Bazooka Labs revenue and deployed into liquidity pools by acquiring project tokens and pairing them with Magnet. This means the treasury acts as a liquidity deployment engine rather than a Magnet accumulator, keeping incentives focused on market depth and ecosystem health.
